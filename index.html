About the Authors

Dr. Gourav Kumar Vani is serving as Assistant Professor (Agricultural Economics) in the Department of Agricultural Economics and Farm Management, JNKVV, Jabalpur. He is also serving as Principal Investigator, Madhya Pradesh Agriculture Market Intelligence Centre, Jabalpur. He has previously been associated as Co-Principal Investigator to project “An Evaluation of Krishi Upaj Mandies for Efficient Marketing of Agricultural Produce in Madhya Pradesh”. He has published several research papers in the field of Agricultural Economics in particular and Economics in general. He has co-authored Agriculture Atlas of the State of Madhya Pradesh (Part-I and II Dynamic).

Ms. Aradhana Singh Rajpoot is Senior Research Fellow in Madhya Pradesh Agriculture Market Intelligence Centre, Jabalpur. She has previously worked under project “An Evaluation of Krishi Upaj Mandies for Efficient Marketing of Agricultural Produce in Madhya Pradesh”.
