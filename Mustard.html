7  Mustard (Sarso)

In the State of Madhya Pradesh, total arrivals of Mustard during 2018 were 797.194 000’ quintals. The highest arrivals of 125.171 000’ quintals were reported in the district of Bhind and the lowest reported arrivals of 0.039 000’ quintals were in the district of Khargone. The average price of Mustard in the State of Madhya Pradesh was Rs. 3419.57 per quintals. The maximum price of Rs. 3660.11 per quintal was reported from the district of Harda and the minimum price of Rs. 2709.16 per quintal was reported from the district of Mandla.

In the State of Madhya Pradesh, total arrivals of Mustard during 2019 were 1833.31 000’ quintals. The highest arrivals of 236.858 000’ quintals were reported in the district of Chhatarpur and the lowest reported arrivals of 0.09 000’ quintals were in the district of Balaghat. The average price of Mustard in the State of Madhya Pradesh was Rs. 3375.73 per quintals. The maximum price of Rs. 3590.34 per quintal was reported from the district of Mandla and the minimum price of Rs. 1925.46 per quintal was reported from the district of Indore.

In the State of Madhya Pradesh, total arrivals of Mustard during 2020 were 2308.13 000’ quintals. The highest arrivals of 217.898 000’ quintals were reported in the district of Morena and the lowest reported arrivals of 0.011 000’ quintals were in the district of Burhanpur. The average price of Mustard in the State of Madhya Pradesh was Rs. 3937.1 per quintals. The maximum price of Rs. 4832.36 per quintal was reported from the district of Narsinghpur and the minimum price of Rs. 0 per quintal was reported from the district of Anuppur.

In the State of Madhya Pradesh, total arrivals of Mustard during 2021 were 4176.65 000’ quintals. The highest arrivals of 719.962 000’ quintals were reported in the district of Morena and the lowest reported arrivals of 0.01 000’ quintals were in the district of Singrauli. The average price of Mustard in the State of Madhya Pradesh was Rs. 5836.14 per quintals. The maximum price of Rs. 7000 per quintal was reported from the district of Barwani and the minimum price of Rs. 1783.51 per quintal was reported from the district of Khandwa.

In the State of Madhya Pradesh, total production of Mustard during 2018-19 was 1040754 tonnes. The highest production of 494149 tonnes were reported in the district of Bhind and the lowest reported production of 0 tonnes were in the district of Barwani.

In the State of Madhya Pradesh, total production of Mustard during 2019-20 was 1038355 tonnes. The highest production of 315158 tonnes were reported in the district of Bhind and the lowest reported production of 0 tonnes were in the district of Barwani.

In the State of Madhya Pradesh, total production of Mustard during 2020-21 was 1307929 tonnes. The highest production of 447455 tonnes were reported in the district of Bhind and the lowest reported production of 0 tonnes were in the district of Alirajpur .
